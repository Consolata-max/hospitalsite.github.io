Kiria-ini Mission Hospital

We Treat God Heals!

Kiria-ini Mission Hospital is owned and managed by the Catholic Diocese of Murang’a. It was started by the Consolata sisters in 1955 as a small dispensary then developed to a maternity wing and later to a general hospital with a capacity of 72 beds. Since inception, the Hospital has grown over the years and currently has an official Government approved bed capacity of 100 distributed in the following wards; Gynecology and maternity, pediatric, Medical wards and surgical rooms and private/amenity ward. It is situated in Mathioya sub-County, Murang’a County. The Hospital is located at Kiria-ini Market and connected to three tarmac roads heading to Murang’a, Kangema and Nyeri respectively. The nearest main hospitals are Murang’a District Hospital and Nyeri provincial General Hospital, at a distance of 32km and 25km from us respectively. Othaya and Kangema sub district hospitals, which are about 10km and 13km respectively, have been upgraded to a District Hospital.
